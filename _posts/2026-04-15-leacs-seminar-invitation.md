Join my LEACS Seminar tomorrow, **17/04/2026**, at **13:00 EET**, either in person or online, where I will share my experiences and insights from my three-year PhD program.

The seminar will cover the following topics:

1. Our work with Parisa Kiyasati (incoming PhD student at Gissen) on the impact of short-term vigilance training on visual processing speed.
2. My mathematical and methodological research on trial-to-trial variability in processing speed in the Theory of Visual Attention (TVA).
3. Our collaborative work with Luca Battaglini and Parisa on the role of precision in fixation as a successful strategy for individuals with central vision loss.
