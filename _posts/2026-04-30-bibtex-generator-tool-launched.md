We have added a new tool to the website: **BibTeX Generator**.

This DOI Metadata Tool lets you generate clean BibTeX from one DOI or many. You can paste DOI values, DOI URLs, or raw identifiers, and the tool will normalize them and build BibTeX entries.

Try it here:

- [BibTeX Generator](/bibtex-generator/)

The tool works with trusted external metadata sources and is designed to keep output clean and practical for citation workflows.
